Introduction

Made With Love

Situated alongside the San Diego Coast, Made With Love offers guests world-class cocktails and creative American Food that is simply irresistable. From the comfortable ambiance to the mellow rock jams, this lounge is your perfect spot to grab a drink (or two or three or four!) with good family and friends! The Richey Family values quality time together while sipping a tasty cocktail and we invite you to join us in laughing and relaxing.

Target audience

The people who visit this site are legally allowed to consume alcohol and looking for a trendy spot to grab a drink whether it's in their area or visiting from far away.

This site will be used to primarily view the speciality cocktails offered at Made With Love so customers can decide if they want to dine with us or not. The small food menu can be viewed along with a gallery to showcase the welcoming aesthetic and vibe of the cocktail lounge.
